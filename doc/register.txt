1. Distribution conditions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
PARCP is Shareware NOT public domain. Please feel free to evaluate
PARCP for up to four weeks. If at the end of this period you can't
bring yourself to delete PARCP you must register.

PARCP may be freely distributed by PD/Shareware libraries and BBS's
so long as PARCP and its documentation always remain together and
complete with all its files.

Changes to the program and/or documentation is forbidden. The software
and documentation listed in README.1ST constitute PARCP and is protected
under international Copyright law.


2. How to register
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
You can register PARCP either at BMT Micro www.bmtmicro.com or better yet
directly with me.

The shareware fee is 20 USD (PayPal) or 16 EUR (SEPA direct wire transfer).
My PayPal e-mail address and IBAN plus BIC/SWIFT is online at
http://joy.sophics.cz/parcp/register.htm


3. Why to register?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
After sending the Shareware fee you will receive a personal keycode.
This keycode together with your name is entered into the PARCP.CFG
configuration file of the Client PARCP. After registering PARCP unlocks all
its new and powerful features to you (see at end of this text).

The reward for paying the Shareware fee is PARCP's continued development.
The extent to which PARCP is upgraded is largely dependent upon the
Shareware response from users. On receiving your Shareware fee and address
you automatically become a registered user.


4. PARCP updates
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
As soon as you are registered, (until further notice) all updates are free,
provided that you get the latest version yourself. Just install the new
(Shareware) version over the old one. You can find the latest Shareware
version of PARCP at ftp and http addresses written below. If you can't
get the PARCP from an internet site, you can send me a blank floppy + $3
for postage costs. I would put the latest PARCP onto the floppy and send it
back to you.

PARCP undergoes continual development and as a registered user your wish
list will be given careful consideration. If you find a bug or have any
suggestions or comments to make please get in touch. I will do my best to
remove bugs.

6. Difference between shareware and registered PARCP
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

After entering your name and personal key into configuration file of PARCP
the following PARCP features become available:

  - unlimited configurable number of items in directory listing (in
    shareware version it's limited to 10 items only)

  - batch file transfers (scripting) - automatic transferring of files

  - special Archive Mode for easy back-ups of your files

  - and also e-mail support, of course

Besides all those features you get nice warm feeling in your heart that you
supported further development of PARCP...
